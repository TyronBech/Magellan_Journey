The reason of Magellan's expedition is to primarily
gather spices and herbs to the spice island
(Moluccas). Magellan wanted a new route from the
west passing Atlantic Ocean, South America to
Pacific Ocean because he believes that the Earth
is round and not flat. So proving the roundness of
the Earth is also his goal. Also Spaniards want to
expand its territory and spread Christianity.
