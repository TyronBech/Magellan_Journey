Rajah Humabon, also known as Hamabar,
is the king (rajah) of the kingdom of
Singhapala (now part of Cebu City).
He is the son of Sri Bantug and the
grandson of Sri Lumay, who came from
Sumatra (now part of Indonesia). Thus,
we can say that the Cebuanos today are
directly related with the Sumatrans.
A marker, located a short distance
from Rajah Humabon's statue, contains
inscription of a brief historical
accounts about Rajah Humabon.
