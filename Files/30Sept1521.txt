The explorers went bacto Palawan from
Brunei, their ships encountered a small
flotilla of junks led by the ruler of
Palawan at the cape of Palawan.
Desparate to replenish their provisions,
the expedition captured the junks and
held Tuan Mahamud hostage. On October
1, 1521 Tuan Mahamud, together with his
brother swore to keep their frienship
before the name of God and the Virgin
Mary.
