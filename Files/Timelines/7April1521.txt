On April 7, 1521 the explorers met the 
rajah of Cebu Rajah Humabon. Humabon
treated Magellan as ordinary traders
and in their first meeting, both parties
talked about extensive relationship
through trade, exhibition of weapons and
feast. April 14, 1521 was the day of
first Christian baptism.
