On the evening of March 27, 1521 explorers
sight a fire in the island of Mazaua or 
Limasawa. They've been approached by the
boats of Rajah Colambu and have a
conversation with the mother tongue of the
expedition Enrique de Malacca who is a 
pre-Hispanic Filipino.
