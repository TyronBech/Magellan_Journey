On March 29, 1521, Holy Friday, they met
Rajah Siaui, the rajah of Butuan and
Caraga who also in Limasawa to hunt with
his brother. Also that day is the
earliest blood compact between explorers
and locals. Two days after, the crew
celebrated the earliest recorded
Christian celebration on the Philippine
territory with two rajahs.
