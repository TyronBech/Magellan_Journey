These rulers of Butuan and Caraga, respectively,
welcomed Magellan's crew when they arrived in
Homonhon (Samar). They established friendly
relations and participated in a blood compact
ceremony. The ceremony called casicasi or blood
compact is done for forging alliances, cheifs
had to undergo this ritual. They are also present
in the Philippine's first mass on March 31, 1521
Sunday.
