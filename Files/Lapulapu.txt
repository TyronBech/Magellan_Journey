Lapu-Lapu or in early records Cilapulapu was
the cheiftain and ruler of Mactan in Visayas
region in the Philippines. He was considered
to be the first hero of the country although
there are arguments that he was just a warior
who fought Spaniards but other Philippine hero
such as Jose Rizal used Lapu-Lapu to be the
icon of the Philippines as brave warrior who
can fight for its colony.
